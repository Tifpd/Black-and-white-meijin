Black and White Meijin

Rules & Format

A regular series of online Gomoku tournaments.

When: Every Friday 20:15 (Warsaw time)

Where: PlayOK.com

Settings: 9 rounds, 5 min + 3 s

League System

1st Half: Fight for the Black Meijin (BM) title.

2nd Half: Fight for the White Meijin (WM) title.

The BM and WM winners will face off at the end of the year for the ultimate Black and White Meijin title.
